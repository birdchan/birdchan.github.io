# Bottled Water

Ever wonder what's the big deal about bottled water? Besides the environmental issue, sometimes depending on where you live the tap water may even be cleaner than the bottled water. We are educated/influenced to believe that bottled water is cleaner and sometimes even tastier. Guess who is behind this? Bingo, the bottled water manufacturers.
