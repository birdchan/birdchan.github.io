# TextMate drawer disappeared...

Sometimes after working on a few [TextMate](http://macromates.com/) windows, one of them will have its file directory tree drawer missing. I don't know why this happens but to bring back the drawer, just go to the menu bar and click View -> Show Project Drawer.
