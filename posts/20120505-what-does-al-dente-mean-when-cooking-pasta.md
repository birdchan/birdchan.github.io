# What does 

![](/images/pasta_dish.jpg)Al dente is a fancy term for pasta that's fully cooked, but not overly soft. The phrase is Italian for "to the tooth," which comes from testing the pasta's consistency with your teeth. [[wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_dente)
